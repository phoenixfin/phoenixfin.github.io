Cold wind breeze slow and bare

Sincere silence creeps into the atmosphere

Pure tranquil fills the air

Dark horizon gives off the stare

The remnants of a wounded pair

Once connected with a simple prayer

<br>

Yet now, he still sits eagerly there

Protects the dimension with honest care

Standby patiently while fully aware

As far as she went off somewhere

And face more the life absurd and unfair

It will always be a place to share

To rest, to heal, or just to shed a tear

<br>

Real world or hidden dimension, he doesn't care

She'll be back or not, he just prepares

If the cared ones need him in some year

As for all the pain she still bears

He believes, nothing is beyond repair
